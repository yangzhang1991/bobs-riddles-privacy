Privacy Policy — Bob's Riddles

Last updated: June 2026

Bob's Riddles is a premium, offline puzzle game. We believe your puzzling should stay between you and Bob.

Data Collection

Bob's Riddles does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app:

  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, location, or any other personal information

Local Storage

Your settings (such as music and sound volume) are stored locally on your device only, using Apple's standard preferences system. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties, because no data is collected in the first place.

Children's Privacy

Bob's Riddles is suitable for all ages and complies with children's privacy regulations by collecting no data whatsoever.
